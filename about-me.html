My name is Sachin and I'm a full-stack web developer with a knack for problem-solving.

I used to be a teacher. It was something I loved to do. In my time in the classroom, I taught students of all ages, from as young as pre-school all the way through adult learners.

I wasn't your average teacher. I started as an ESL instructor. That journey began in Nepal and India and continued for many years on a beautiful island called Jeju in South Korea.

Moving Back to Chicago

After many years abroad, my wife and I decided it was time to move back home in 2016. It was not an easy transition, but we both lucked out getting teaching jobs when we returned to Chicago.

We both transitioned from teaching ESL to teaching STEM.

Teaching STEM was inspirational

I loved teaching STEM, but my wife quickly moved on to follow her dream of completing a Masters in Clinical Psychology.

I became Director of Education for an after-school STEM provider. There, I started to do amazing things. We taught elementary school students how to build DIY robots, drones and much more. I also found a new appreciation and fascination with coding.

Changing Careers

My wife had already made her big change, and now it was my turn.

I decided to hone my skills by joining Flatiron School full time. There, I learned some amazing new skills with a focus on full-stack web development. The journey wasn't easy. But neither is moving across the globe. Now, I am finally ready to kick off this next chapter in my life as a full-stack web developer. I can't wait to see what challenges and opportunites lie ahead.
